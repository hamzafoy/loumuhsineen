Louisville Muhsineen

The Louisville Muhsineen is a gathering of Muslim men ranging from ages 18 to 45 who cooperate with one another in developing themselves towards being foremost in their knowledge, practice, & adherence to Islamic principles, worthy sons, husbands & fathers, & most beneficial to our local community. We dedicate ourselves to 'ilm (عِلْم), 'ibadah (عِبَادَة), & 'amaal (عَمَل) & promote sound masculinity rooted in the Qu'ran & the Sunnah.
